Our Process

Innovation and Advancement

  • Brief

    We begin by knowing the client's brief on the project and work together until its conclusion to ensure that all of the stated requirements are met.

  • Design

    We create mockups for websites during the design process, receive customer permission on the layout, and then go on to the production phase.

  • Development

    During this phase, we host and build the complete website, do re-runs to ensure that everything is in order, and correct any issues. Following the conclusion of this step, we get feedback from the customer.

  • Finalize

    Finally, we launch the website, align it with the brief, and show it to the customer. We do accommodate modifications as long as it is part of the brief that we have overlooked. We execute the transaction only our customer is entirely happy.
